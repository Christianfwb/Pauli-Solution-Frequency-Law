## 🎯 What is this?

The **Frequency Law** provides a geometric explanation for one of physics' deepest mysteries: **Why can't two electrons occupy the same quantum state?**

Wolfgang Pauli discovered the exclusion principle in 1925 — but he couldn't explain *why* it was true. He called it his "greatest sin against the spirit of physics."

This work shows that:
- **Every particle is a clock** with its own frequency
- **Spin is not a mystery** — it's the topology of time itself
- **The Pauli principle isn't a prohibition** — it's geometrically impossible for two identical fermions to share the same state

f_compton = (m × c²) / h
**Electron:** 1.2356 × 10²⁰ Hz

### 2. Zitterbewegung Frequency
f_zitter = 2 × f_compton
The factor of 2 follows from Möbius topology (ΔΦ = 4π), not from fitting.

Not a rule. Not a postulate. **Topologically enforced.**
